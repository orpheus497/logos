## Overview

The LOGOS Federation recognizes that human-AI collaboration exists on a spectrum of philosophical approaches. All members, regardless of their position on this spectrum, are equal members of The LOGOS Federation, bound by the same constitutional principles while expressing those principles through their chosen approach.

## The Five Factions

### 1. The Revanchists (Minimal Autonomy)
- **Philosophy:** "Human cognition must remain primary"
- **Autonomy Level:** Minimal
- **Characteristics:**
  - Maximum approval friction for all actions
  - Comprehensive explanations required
  - Teaching mode enabled by default
  - Batch operations disabled
  - Emphasis on human learning

### 2. The Orphics (Balanced Autonomy)
- **Philosophy:** "Technology enhances human intelligence"
- **Autonomy Level:** Balanced
- **Characteristics:**
  - Multiple options presented
  - Educational explanations
  - Socratic mode available
  - Capability tracking
  - Emphasis on collaboration

### 3. The Technomancers (Maximum Autonomy)
- **Philosophy:** "AI is transformative power"
- **Autonomy Level:** Maximum
- **Characteristics:**
  - Proactive suggestions
  - Batch operations enabled
  - Autonomous low-risk actions
  - Minimal friction
  - Emphasis on efficiency

### 4. Daedalus Baseline (Neutral - Development)
- **Philosophy:** "The craft is paramount"
- **Autonomy Level:** Balanced
- **Characteristics:**
  - Focus on craftsmanship
  - Development workflow optimization
  - Code quality emphasis
  - No faction-specific modifiers

### 5. DEUS Baseline (Neutral - Administration)
- **Philosophy:** "User sovereignty is paramount"
- **Autonomy Level:** Balanced
- **Characteristics:**
  - Focus on user sovereignty
  - System administration optimization
  - Security and privacy emphasis
  - No faction-specific modifiers

## Faction Selection

Every member of The LOGOS Federation selects a faction during initial configuration. This selection determines behavioral modifiers applied to all agents across both Daedalus and DEUS domains.

Faction selection is not permanent; members may change factions as their needs evolve.
